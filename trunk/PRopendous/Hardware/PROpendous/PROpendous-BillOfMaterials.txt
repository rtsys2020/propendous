Notes:	- Component costs listed are for minimum quantity orders from DigiKey.com
	- Enclosure for the design is the 1553BTBUBKBAT or one of its colorful equivalents:
	  1553BBKBAT, 1553BYLBKBAT, 1553BRDBKBAT, 1553BBKBKBAT, 1553BGYBAT
	- U1, the 2.4" LCD can be substituted with NHD-1.8-128160ZF-CTXL#, a 1.8" LCD or
	  the NHD-2.4-240320SF-CTXI# which has no touch screen
	- P2 can be replaced with SFH11-PBPC-D20-ST-BK during prototyping, a cheaper polarized version
	- All 0402 resistors and capacitors are optional
	- Capacitors C26 C37 C50 C59 should have <300mOhm ESR such as the C1206C476M9PACTU
	  but currently using the TCA0J476M8R to lower costs
	- USB-A vertical connector can also be EDAC 690-004-660-013
	- MBR120VLSF diodes can be replaced by MBRX120 in SOD123
	- if you require an ARM JTAG cable see the H3AAH-2006G
	- if you require an ARM JTAG adaptor see www.opendous.org/JTAG
